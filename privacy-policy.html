KCS Business Consultancy ("KCS," "we," "our," or "us") is a professional consultancy firm based in Indore, Madhya Pradesh. We are committed to protecting the privacy and confidentiality of all personal information entrusted to us by our clients, website visitors, and partners. This Privacy Policy explains our practices in plain language.

01

Information We Collect

When you interact with KCS — through our website, consultation booking form, or direct communication — we may collect the following types of information:

👤

Personal Identifiers

Full name, email address, phone number, and company name provided via our contact or booking forms.

📅

Appointment Details

Preferred meeting date, time slot, meeting type or reason for consultation, and any additional notes you choose to share.

💼

Business Information

Details about your business, industry, operational challenges, and goals shared during consultations or communications.

🌐

Usage Data

Browser type, IP address, pages visited, time spent on site, and referral sources collected automatically when you visit our website.

02

How We Use Your Information

KCS uses the information we collect solely for legitimate business and operational purposes:

  • Scheduling & Consultations: To confirm, manage, and conduct your requested meetings and consultations.
  • Service Delivery: To understand your business needs and provide tailored consulting strategies, analyses, and recommendations.
  • Communication: To respond to your inquiries, follow up after meetings, and send relevant updates about our services.
  • Improvement: To analyze website usage and improve our online presence, user experience, and service offerings.
  • Legal Compliance: To meet our obligations under applicable Indian laws and regulations.
We do not use your data for unsolicited marketing or sell it to any third party for advertising purposes.
03

Sharing of Information

KCS treats your information with strict confidentiality. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third-party tools that support our operations (e.g., scheduling platforms, email services), bound by confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal Requirements: When required to comply with applicable law, a court order, or governmental authority in India.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or restructuring of KCS, subject to equivalent privacy protections.
  • With Your Consent: In any other circumstance, only with your explicit prior consent.
04

Data Storage & Security

We implement reasonable and appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These include:

  • Secure, encrypted communication channels (HTTPS) for all data transmission.
  • Access controls limiting data access to authorized KCS personnel only.
  • Regular review of our data handling practices.

Your data is retained for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by applicable law. Once no longer needed, data is securely deleted or anonymized.

05

Cookies & Tracking

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience and collect usage analytics. Types of cookies we may use:

Type Purpose Duration
Essential Required for the website to function properly Session
Analytics Understand how visitors use our site (e.g., Google Analytics) Up to 2 years
Preference Remember your settings and preferences 1 year

You may disable cookies via your browser settings; however, some features of the website may not function as intended if you do so.

06

Your Rights

As an individual whose data we process, you have the following rights with respect to your personal information:

Access

Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

Correction

Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.

Deletion

Request deletion of your data, subject to legal retention requirements.

Objection

Object to certain processing of your data, including for marketing purposes.

Portability

Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

Withdrawal

Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at kcsbusiness.me@gmail.com. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

07

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external websites such as LinkedIn, Instagram, Facebook, or other platforms. KCS is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these third-party sites. We encourage you to review their respective privacy policies before sharing any personal information.

08

Children's Privacy

KCS services are designed for businesses and business professionals. Our website is not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected data from a minor, we will promptly delete it.

09

Changes to This Policy

KCS reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal obligations. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of our website or services after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
